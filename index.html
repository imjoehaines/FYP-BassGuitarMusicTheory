Notes on the fretboard

This exercise will help you to learn the notes on the fretboard. In the exercise a fret will be highlighted with a red dot, you need to select the note at this fret by clicking the corresponding button at the bottom of the screen.

+
Intervals

This exercise will help you to learn the 12 basic intervals. Intervals are one of the key pieces of knowledge needed to learn chords and scales. They are also incredibly useful shorthand when talking to other musicians. In the exercise the root note will be highlighted with a red dot and the desired interval will be displayed at the bottom of the screen. You need to select the note that represents the interval from the root note.

+
Scales

This exercise will help you to learn scales, which are incredibly important when working with other musicians. In the exercise the root note will be highlighted with a red dot and a random note from the chosen scale will be displayed at the bottom of the screen. You need to select the desired note from the fretboard.
